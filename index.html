❓ FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before joining. Still have questions? Join our Discord.

How do I actually earn the 30-65% APY? +

You earn rewards by validating transactions on the BLending Financial Loan-Swap™ platform. Our mobile app sends you push notifications when validation is needed (typically 3-5 times per day). You review the transaction data, and if it looks correct, you approve it. For each successful validation, you earn a portion of the platform's transaction fees. Your APY is determined by your validator tier—higher tiers stake more BTC and earn higher returns.

Is my Bitcoin safe? Can I withdraw it? +

Yes! Your BTC is staked in our audited smart contract (audited by CertiK and OpenZeppelin). You maintain custody—we never hold your keys. You can withdraw anytime with a 7-day cooldown period. This cooldown exists to prevent manipulation and maintain network security. Your stake is never at risk from platform insolvency since it's locked in the smart contract, not in our hands.

What happens if I miss validation checks? +

Missing occasional checks is fine—life happens! However, if you miss 3+ validations in a 7-day period, you'll receive a temporary reputation penalty that reduces your rewards by 10% for the next 7 days. Miss 10+ in a month and you'll be temporarily suspended. The good news? 95% of validators never hit these limits because our mobile app makes it easy to validate on the go.

How does the reputation system work? +

Every new validator starts at 50% reputation weight, meaning you earn 50% of your tier's max rewards initially. For every 30 days of consistent performance (95%+ validation success rate), your reputation increases by 25% until you hit 100% at day 90. Higher reputation = higher rewards. Your reputation also increases faster if you catch anomalies or maintain perfect uptime.

Can I upgrade my tier later? +

Absolutely! You can upgrade anytime by staking additional BTC. For example, if you start as a Scout (0.05 BTC, 30% APY) and stake another 0.15 BTC, you instantly become a Guardian (0.20 BTC, 48% APY). Your reputation score carries over, so you don't lose any progress. Many validators start small to test the waters and upgrade within the first month.

Why are the returns so high compared to traditional staking? +

Great question! Traditional staking (Ethereum, Cardano) pays 4-6% because validators only provide computational security. Guardian validators provide THREE things: (1) Computational infrastructure, (2) Economic security through stake, and (3) Human intelligence to catch anomalies algorithms miss. You're essentially being paid for fraud prevention, not just computation. The Loan-Swap™ platform generates $10M+ annually in fees, and we share 35% of that with validators.

How do I increase my chances of getting accepted? +

We prioritize: (1) Geographic diversity (underrepresented regions get preference), (2) Commitment signals (detailed application, Discord activity), (3) Technical competence (previous validator experience helps but isn't required), and (4) Community engagement (referrals from existing Guardians). Pro tip: Join our Discord before applying and participate in discussions. Active community members move to the front of the waitlist.

What if BTC price crashes? Do I lose money? +

Your BTC stake and your BTC rewards are denominated in BTC, not USD. So if you stake 0.20 BTC as a Guardian, you'll earn approximately 0.096 BTC per year (48% APY), regardless of BTC's USD price. If BTC crashes, your USD value drops, but you still have more BTC than you started with. If BTC pumps, your gains are even bigger. Think of it as "earning yield on your Bitcoin" rather than converting to dollars.
